The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Nefarious

This is my favorite scary movie. It's about an atheist therapist interviewing a demon-possessed man.

Sound of Freedom

This is my favorite thriller movie. It's about how a man takes a leap of faith to make a difference ending child trafficking.

The Lion, The Witch, and The Wardrobe

This is a metaphoric movie about the gospel.
